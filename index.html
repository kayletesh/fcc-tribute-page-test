Timeline of Grey's Call's life

(Dates Not Canon)

Ac: After the Creation(References the creation of the Calm Waters).

  • 1272ac - last-bloom: In late last-bloom, a dust gray kit with a black paw is born to a litter of four.
  • 1273ac - full-bloom: Grey and siblings choose the paths to which they will train as 'first steps'.
  • 1274ac - full-bloom: Grey and surviving two siblings recieve their 'final steps' ceremony.
  • 1275ac - first-bloom: Grey earns his true name after a costly fight against the colony cats.
  • 1275ac - last-bloom: Juniper's Call uses his final gift to defeat the colony cat's leader.
  • 1275ac - no-bloom: Rabbits and mice start dying in the grass fields.
  • 1276ac - first-bloom: By early first-bloom, half the tribe is sick. The only safe prey are fish, and only a quarter of the remaining cats are fit to hunt.
  • 1276ac - first-bloom: Juniper's Call falls ill. Grey's Claw receives a vision from Koa.
  • 1276ac - first-bloom: Grey's Claw sets out on a journey, following Koa's path to find a cure
  • 1276ac - full-bloom: Grey's Claw discovers the cure with the help of a fae from the Tribe of Shaded Pines. The two begin their journey home.
  • 1276ac - last-bloom: Grey and Magpie return to the Calm Waters, and help the healer distribute the herbs among the tribe.
  • 1278ac - full-bloom: Juniper's Call dies in during a storm. Crey's Claw takes his place as leader, taking the name of Grey's Call.
  • 1280ac - last-bloom:Grey's Call celebrates the birth of his kits, and welcomes a new era for the tribe.
